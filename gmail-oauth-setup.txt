# Gmail OAuth2 Setup for Professional Email Integration

## Why OAuth2 is Better Than App Passwords
✅ More secure (no hardcoded passwords)
✅ Professional appearance (sent by your domain)
✅ Higher sending limits
✅ Better deliverability
✅ No "via less secure app" warnings

## Step 1: Create Google Cloud Project
1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/
2. Sign in with: dclayborn@visitingangels.com
3. Click "Select a project" → "New Project"
4. Project name: "Clayborn Group Website"
5. Click "Create"

## Step 2: Enable Gmail API
1. In your project, go to "APIs & Services" → "Library"
2. Search for "Gmail API"
3. Click on "Gmail API"
4. Click "Enable"
5. Go to "APIs & Services" → "OAuth consent screen"
6. Choose "External" → "Create"

## Step 3: Configure OAuth Consent Screen
1. **App name**: "Clayborn Group Website"
2. **User support email**: "dclayborn@visitingangels.com"
3. **Application logo**: (optional - upload your logo)
4. **Application homepage**: "https://theclayborngroup.com"
5. **Application privacy policy**: "https://theclayborngroup.com/privacy"
6. **Application terms of service**: "https://theclayborngroup.com/terms"
7. **Authorized domains**: "theclayborngroup.com"
8. **Developer contact information**: "dclayborn@visitingangels.com"
9. Click "Save and Continue"

## Step 4: Configure OAuth Scopes
1. Click "Add or Remove Scopes"
2. Add these scopes:
   - `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send`
   - `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email`
3. Click "Update"

## Step 5: Create Credentials
1. Go to "APIs & Services" → "Credentials"
2. Click "+ Create Credentials" → "OAuth client ID"
3. **Application type**: "Web application"
4. **Name**: "Clayborn Group Web Client"
5. **Authorized JavaScript origins**: 
   - "http://localhost:3001"
   - "https://theclayborngroup.com"
6. **Authorized redirect URIs**:
   - "http://localhost:3001/api/auth/callback"
   - "https://theclayborngroup.com/api/auth/callback"
7. Click "Create"

## Step 6: Get Your Credentials
You'll receive:
- **Client ID**: (copy this)
- **Client Secret**: (copy this - keep secure!)

## Step 7: Update Environment Variables
Create `.env.local` with:

```env
GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID=your-client-id-here
GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET=your-client-secret-here
GOOGLE_REDIRECT_URI=http://localhost:3001/api/auth/callback
GMAIL_USER=dclayborn@visitingangels.com
```

## Benefits of OAuth2 Setup
✅ Professional email appearance
✅ Higher sending quotas
✅ Better security
✅ No "less secure app" warnings
✅ Scalable for production
✅ Easy to manage permissions

## Testing
Once configured, test your contact form at:
http://localhost:3001/contact

## Production Deployment
For production deployment:
1. Add your production domain to authorized origins
2. Update redirect URI to production URL
3. Deploy to Vercel/Netlify
4. Test production contact form
